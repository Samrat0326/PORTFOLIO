Secure Web Development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Welcome to My Portpolio ! We are a dedicated resource for developers, students, and IT professionals looking to master the essential principles of front-end cybersecurity using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between web development and security, empowering developers to build robust, secure, and resilient web applications. We believe that security is a non-negotiable aspect of modern website development and should be an ongoing priority, not an afterthought.

Why Trust Us?

We prioritize accuracy and relevance, aligning our content with industry best practices and standards from reputable sources like the OWASP Cheat Sheet Series. Our goal is to ensure you have the knowledge and tools needed to safeguard your digital presence and protect both your applications and your users.
